Favourite Places

Taj Mahal

An immense mausoleum of white marble, built in Agra between 1631 and 1648 by order of the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan.

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple is an open house of worship for all people, from all walks of life and faiths.

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace, also known as Amba Vilas Palace, is a historical palace and a royal residence.
